Welcome to My Website!

This portfolio project was created for my Bellevue College Web Development class in March 2025 and features some cool projects I completed during the quarter and outside of school. Plus, on this site you can learn a little something about me! There is also an option to shoot me an email, so be sure to reach out with any questions and inquiries.

The theme of this site is office supplies. I chose this because since I was a kid, I have loved to tinker with everything design related. I curated my own bulletin boards, made my own stickers, and drew in countless journals. Now that I have the ability to create websites and apps, this site pays homage to 10-year-old Kai's creativity. Enjoy!
